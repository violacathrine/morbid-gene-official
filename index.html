Melodic death metal had a smoking-hot love affair with groove metal, and gave birth to a beautiful bastard known as Morbid Gene. This Stockholm-based band has sworn a sacred vow to max out adrenaline levels on stage and conjure tunes that will gladly stay on repeat until death.

Latest news

06.02.25 - We recently had a gig at The Node in Stockholm, where we participated in the Emergenza Festival music competition. Thanks to our dedicated fans, friends, and family who voted for us, we came in first place and are thrilled to continue being part of Emergenza's competition. Next up is the semi-final at Slakthuset in Stockholm, and we hope everyone will come and support us then as well. We are so grateful for all the love and support we have received so far, and we can't wait to see what the future holds for Morbid Gene.
